Intersting Facts About Camel

  • There are two types of camels: One humped or “dromedary” camels and two humped Bactrian camels.
  • Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes.
  • Camels have thick lips which let them forage for thorny plants other animals can't eat.

Intersting Facts About Chameleon

  • Chameleons are reptiles that are a part of iguana suborder.
  • Changing skin color is an important part of communication among chameleons.
  • Most Chameleons have a prehensile tail that they use to wrap around tree branches.

Intersting Facts About Pigeon

  • Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals.
  • Pigeons are highly sociable animals.
  • Pigeons are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities.

Intersting Facts About Panda

  • Giant pandas are good at climbing trees and can also swim.
  • Pandas have so many fans because they look cute.
  • An adult can eat 12–38 kilos of bamboo per day!

Intersting Facts About Teddy

  • The Teddy Bears' Picnic song was originally called The Teddy Bear Two Step.
  • In 1902 in Germany, Steiff launched “Bear 55 PB”, the first toy bear with jointed arms and legs.
  • The Oxford English Dictionary dates the first use of the term teddy bear to 1906.

Intersting Facts About Cicada

  • The loud whirring or buzzing sound you hear is an all-male cicada chorus.
  • Their short adult life is not unusual for bugs.
  • Most have red-orange eyes.
